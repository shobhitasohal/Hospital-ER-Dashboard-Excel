# Hospital Emergency Room Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end data analytics project transforming raw hospital emergency room data into an interactive Excel dashboard for hospital leadership to monitor patient flow, wait times, and satisfaction performance.

**Tools used:** Excel (Power Query · Power Pivot · DAX · Pivot Tables · Pivot Charts)

## Data Source
Hospital emergency room operational data covering 9,000+ patient records across departments, referrals, wait times, and satisfaction scores.

---

## About This Project

End-to-end solo project covering data ingestion, data modelling, KPI development, and dashboard design — executed entirely within Excel. The dashboard was built for a non-technical executive audience and designed to support operational decision-making across staffing, patient flow, and departmental performance.

---

## Business Problem

Hospital emergency rooms are complex environments where managing patient flow, minimizing wait times, and ensuring high patient satisfaction are critical operational priorities. Without a centralized, data-driven view of ER operations, hospital leadership was unable to identify bottlenecks, allocate staff effectively, or understand which patient groups were most underserved.

The goal was to design and deliver a comprehensive, interactive Excel dashboard that transforms raw ER operational data into clear, actionable intelligence — covering patient flow, demographics, departmental referrals, wait times, and satisfaction scores.

---

## Approach

The analysis was structured across four stages, executed entirely within Excel:

- **Data ingestion and preparation** — raw ER data was imported and transformed using Power Query, resolving missing values, standardizing formats, and structuring records for downstream analysis
- **Data modelling** — a relational model was built across multiple tables in Power Pivot, enabling cross-functional calculations and multi-dimensional analysis
- **KPI development** — custom DAX measures were written to quantify average wait time by hour and day, percentage of delayed services, satisfaction scores by department and age group, and referral volume by department and gender
- **Executive dashboard** — an interactive two-page dashboard was built with dynamic year and month slicers, enabling leadership to interrogate any time period for targeted operational review

## Key Findings

- **62% of services were experiencing delays** — precipitating a hospital-wide initiative to identify and eliminate operational bottlenecks
- **Wait times peaked at 44 minutes around noon** — enabling targeted staffing interventions during critical demand hours
- **Below-average satisfaction scores in Cardiology and Gastroenterology** — escalated for immediate root cause analysis and process redesign
- **General Practice and Orthopedics identified as primary ER referral pathways** — prioritized for capacity planning and workflow optimization
- **Age groups 40–49 and 70+ recorded the lowest satisfaction scores** — highlighted for dedicated patient experience improvement initiatives
- **Dynamic time-based filtering** enabled management to monitor performance across any month or year, supporting agile and evidence-based decision making
